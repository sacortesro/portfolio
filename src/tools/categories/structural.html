PDB
Protein Database

The Protein Data Bank (PDB) is a repository for the 3D structural data of large biological molecules, such as proteins and nucleic acids. I use PDB for structure retrieval, visualization, and analysis.

Key Applications:

  • 3D structure retrieval
  • Visualization of protein structures
  • Analysis of protein-ligand interactions
  • Structure-based drug design
